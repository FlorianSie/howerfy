Welcome to howerfy!

howerfy lets you manage your tasks more effectively by following the popular Eisenhower Matrix framework.

Key Functions

Create Tasks
With howerfy you can easily create and keep track of complex tasks. In order to monitor the progress of each item, milestones can be assigned and set to completed once done.

Rate Importance & Urgency
You can rate a tasks’s urgency and importance based on which the task will be assigned to one of the four Eisenhower categories. Once you assign a task to a team, team members will be able to rate a tasks urgency and importance as well. This enables you and your team to manage projects in a transparent and efficent way based on clearly set and agreed priorities.

Create & Manage Teams
You can create teams and assign members to it. All members can create new tasks and assign them to the team as well. Howerfy provides a dashboard that enables you to keep track of your team’s progress.
